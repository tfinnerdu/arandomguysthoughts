## Hey, I'm just a random guy.

I'm a developer, a dad, a gamer, a cook, and — like most guys — someone who's been through some stuff but doesn't always know how to talk about it.

I started this blog because I think there's a gap in the conversation. We've got plenty of content out there for men about sports, tech, food, and hobbies. And there's plenty of clinical, formal content about mental health. But there's not much that puts it all together in the same place, from the same person, in a way that feels real.

I lost my younger brother to suicide. I'll be honest — it messed me up. It changed how I see the world, how I think about communication, relationships, and what it means to actually be okay versus just saying you are. Depression from a male perspective is something nobody wants to touch. You're "soft" or "weak" if you show feelings. That's garbage, and I think most guys know it — they just don't hear it from other guys enough.

So this is me being that guy. I'm not a therapist. I'm not a life coach. I'm just a dude in Nebraska who smokes brisket, plays video games, watches too much sports, and has some things to say about life.

### What You'll Find Here

- **Things We Don't Talk About** — Mental health, grief, relationships, communication, and the stuff men carry but rarely say out loud.
- **Culinary Delights** — Recipes, BBQ techniques, and the joy of cooking for people you care about.
- **Video Games** — What I'm playing, what I'm thinking about, and the occasional hot take.
- **Sports** — Hot takes, cold takes, and everything in between.
- **Nerdy Stuff** — D&D, esports, tech, and all the gloriously geeky things that make life interesting.
- **Random Thoughts** — Exactly what it sounds like. Life's weird. Let's talk about it.
